Our Story

Born in a Himachali kitchen

"Every jar carries the taste of our mountains, the patience of slow cooking, and the warmth of home."

Shimla Spice was born in our family kitchen, tucked away in the hills of Himachal. What began as small batches of pickles for loved ones soon turned into jars of stories worth sharing. Each recipe carries the taste of our traditions — for us, it's not just about pickles, it's about bottling memories. 💛

Why do we need Pickles?

🦠

Rich in Probiotics

Naturally fermented pickles contain natural probiotics that promote gut health and digestion.

🛡️

Boosts Immunity

Spices, garlic, and ginger strengthen the body's defense system with powerful antioxidants.

✨

Packed with Antioxidants

Vegetables and spices fight free radicals, keeping you healthy and youthful longer.

🍽️

Enhances Appetite

Tangy-sour taste stimulates taste buds and naturally improves your appetite.

❤️

Good for Heart

Ingredients like garlic and mustard seeds actively support long-term heart health.

🌱

Preserves Nutrients

Seasonal veggies retain their vitamins and minerals for longer in homemade pickles.
